The R52

The R52 is my first ever thinkpad. I've got it from a local second-hand seller. It's in good condition, but without a charger. Also the ultrabay came empty and the ram-cover is missing. Some screws are missing but I found the official manual. It should work with a charger, atleast this is what the seller said. There were some photos so I think it's legit. I plan to buy a charger before anything else. Then a battery for the ultrabay or the ram-cover, I'm not sure yet. It'll run Arch (btw). It's rather old so I won't try to install a bloated desktop enviroment just a wm.

The Specs:

  • Pentium M 1.6 Ghz, upgradable not soldered
  • 2GB of DDR2 Ram, max. amount
  • 14 inch Display
  • The nice keyboard with trackpoint and a trackpad.
